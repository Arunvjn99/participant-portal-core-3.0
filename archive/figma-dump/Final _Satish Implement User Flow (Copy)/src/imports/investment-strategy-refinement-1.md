Refine the Investment Strategy screen so that it reflects the user's risk identity and clearly explains the recommended portfolio.

The current layout is visually clean but lacks context and trust signals.

---

RISK PROFILE CONTEXT

At the top show a clear investor identity card.

Example:

Your Investor Profile
Balanced Investor

Description:
A mix of growth and stability designed for long-term retirement investing.

Add a secondary action:

Change Risk Profile

This should allow the user to adjust their risk preference without leaving the screen.

---

RECOMMENDED STRATEGY

Rename the main portfolio section:

Recommended Strategy for Balanced Investors

Add supporting text:

This portfolio is designed to match your risk profile and long-term retirement goals.

---

PORTFOLIO COMPOSITION

Display a donut chart showing the recommended allocation.

Example:

US Stocks – 40%
Bonds – 25%
International Stocks – 20%
Real Estate – 15%

Ensure the chart is visually prominent.

---

PORTFOLIO OUTCOME CONTEXT

Add a short performance context below the allocation.

Example:

Expected long-term annual growth: ~6.8%

or

Projected retirement balance based on this strategy.

This helps users understand why the allocation matters.

---

FUND TRANSPARENCY

Add an expandable section below the portfolio card:

View funds included

When expanded show the actual funds used in the allocation.

Example:

Vanguard Total Stock Market
Vanguard Mid-Cap Index
Vanguard Total Bond Market
International Growth Fund
Vanguard Real Estate Index

---

PRIMARY ACTION

Keep a strong primary CTA:

Use Recommended Portfolio

---

ADVANCED OPTION

Below the recommendation include:

Customize My Portfolio

This option should allow experienced users to manually choose funds and allocations but should remain collapsed by default.

---

FINAL RESULT

The investment step should:

Reconnect the user with their risk profile
Clearly explain the recommended portfolio
Provide transparency into the underlying funds
Offer customization without overwhelming beginner users
