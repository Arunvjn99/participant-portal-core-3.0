Improve the existing Plan Selection screen in the retirement enrollment flow without changing the overall layout or design system.

Add a small informational banner above the plan cards.

Your employer matches contributions up to 6%.

Below the plan cards add a small reassurance line:

• The card becomes highlighted
• The border becomes stronger
• The selection state becomes clear

VISUAL IMPROVEMENTS

• light background tint

FINAL RESULT

The plan selection screen should:

• Clearly present retirement plan options
• Reduce decision anxiety
• Provide social proof instead of forced recommendation
• Prepare users for the contribution step
